Join us!

This is easy!
We at Horizon function on a rotation model: A large clan focusing on having fun along with a few select people taking it to the next level, known as the main roster. This main roster is selected and rotated from the whole clan's top players internally every so often. As of such, anyone can have a shot of becoming part of the main roster and achieving true greatness... in this video game. There are no obligations to try to join the main roster. Requirements for Joining: 1. Having a good attitude toward the game and other players. Horizon is built on humility, determination, and respect. Deliberately ruining others' experience is not tolerated. 2. Reasonable ability in the English language. 3.Details of your ingame ID, Race, and League. To join us please join to our Discord channel #welcome and contact anybody from @Recruitment (brown names). Hope to see you soon! Our Discord
